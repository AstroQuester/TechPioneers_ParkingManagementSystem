Frequently Asked Questions

Below are some of the most frequently asked questions along with their answers.If you still need assistance, please let us know about your dificulty through the contact us portal provided on this webpage.

  • How can I reserve a parking space?

    After logging in, click on the "Book your spot now!" button, select your desired location and time, and complete the reservation process.

  • Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

    Yes, you can manage your reservations from the "My Reservations" section on your profile.

  • What payment methods are accepted for reservations?

    We accept credit/debit cards and various mobile wallets for payment.

  • How can I access the parking facility using QR code?

    Upon making a reservation, you'll receive a QR code. Simply scan it at the entry gate to access the facility.

  • What is the flagship fare option?

    With this option user won't be charged with regular prices instead, will only be charged on the pay-as-you-use basis.
